About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! I am a full stack Web developer with a passion for writing clean, scalable, and sophisticated code. I am a graduate from Rutgers University where I studied Mathematics and Computer Science. There I was a member of multiple acedemic organizations such as the Rutgers Undergraduate Mathematics Association and the Undergraduate Student Alliance of Computer Scientist. During my time in college I developed many interests in Literature, Philosophy, and Cognitive Science but most of all discovered my passion for Mathematics and Computer Science. Before I went to college I spend two years working with adults with developmental disabilities at Alfa Development Inc. There is where I discovered how much I love to help and teach others. I specialize in MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) fullstack development, but I also have years of experience with java, C, Python, and SQL.
